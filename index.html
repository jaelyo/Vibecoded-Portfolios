    ✏️ HOW TO EDIT THIS PORTFOLIO

    1. 1
      Open data.js in any text editor (Notepad, VS Code, TextEdit).
    2. 2
      Replace the [placeholder] text with your real information — name, school, achievements, projects, and more.
    3. 3
      Place your profile photo in images/ and update profilePhoto: in data.js.
    4. 4
      Put achievement/project photos in images/achievements/, images/projects/, or images/robots/ and update each imagePath:.
    5. 5
      Put certificates in images/certificates/ and update each certificatePath:.
    6. 6
      Save data.js, then open (or refresh) index.html in your browser. No internet or server needed!
    7. 7
      To add more achievements or projects, copy and paste an existing block inside data.js and fill in the new details.
